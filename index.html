I am a hearing-impaired person, severely hearing impaired by nature. I usually use lip reading and handwriting to talk. I use basic sign language and listen to communicate with deaf people. Defeated by my shortcomings, I can try to overcome any difficulties. At the beginning of 2011, I and my sister set off together for a 9-month working holiday in Australia. The journey during this period made me more informed and experienced a different completely different way of life in Taiwan than in other cultures.This period made me understand Before doing everything, a thorough plan can make the result more perfect. Working in an Australian business has allowed me to experience the Western people's ideas and life patterns in the field. In August 2012, I participated in "Look! We go around the island by SOZO Beat Hearing impaired journey", 11 days and 10 nights cycling trip around the island. I met many people along the way to cheer up and cheer, and my partners supported and cared for each other and united. With great strength, everyone encourages me along the way, making me more confident that I can fulfill my dream of round the island.
